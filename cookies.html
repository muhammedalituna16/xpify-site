Cookie Policy

XPIFY uses cookies to ensure smooth operation and enhance your experience. This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and how you can control them.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files stored on your device by websites or apps you visit. They help improve functionality and user experience.

2. What Types of Cookies Do We Use?

  • Required Cookies: Essential for the basic functionality of the app.
  • Functional Cookies: Help us remember your preferences and settings.
  • Analytics Cookies: Allow us to analyze visitor behavior and site usage.

3. How Can You Manage Cookies?

You can manage, delete, or block cookies through your browser or device settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect functionality.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Services such as Firebase and Google Analytics may use their own cookies. Their policies can be found on their official websites.

5. Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. All updates will be posted on this page.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about our use of cookies, please contact us via the app.
